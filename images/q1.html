The
Resort

Relax in a Mediterranean moment.

With blush hues and a luscious backdrop, The Resort takes your next outdoor adventure to soaring new heights. Reimagine your dream Mediterrian escape with picturesque views of the ocean from our elegant gondola.
Feel refreshed as you mingle with a cold glass of bubbly and bask in the sun’s radiance at our lounge pier. Enjoy a swift game of bowling like you’ve never before, and marvel in the intricate rock formations of the surrounding cliffs. As dusk draws near, experience a romantic dinner for two, right there in the water with our seaside dining. Absorb in the unique scents and tastes of the European coastline — the quintessential relaxation spot for your getaway.
